About

I am currently a master of public policy graduate student at UCLA. I studied legal studies as an undergraduate at UC Berkeley and then worked as an auditor for Deloitte & Touche LLP. After that, I ran my own independent wedding and portrait photography business before returning to school at UCLA to begin my MPP studies. Since returning to school, I’ve discovered that I really enjoy quantitative methodology, and am passionate about using it to frame and address public policy issues.
